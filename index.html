Amanoi

With Vietnam's stunning coastline stretching beautifully and embraced by Nui Chua National Park, Amanoi appears from afar as a natural paradise overlooking Vinh Hy Bay. From cliff-top restaurants and pools, to the lakeside Aman Spa and private white-sand beach, the resort offers unparalleled opportunities for relaxation, adventure, and discovery.

Rooms & Suites

Each pavilion and villa is thoughtfully designed to blend seamlessly with the natural landscape, offering breathtaking views and unparalleled comfort. From the elegant Pavilion rooms to the expansive private villas, every space reflects the essence of Vietnamese architecture and contemporary luxury.

Getting to Amanoi

Discover Our Location

Amanoi is 75 minutes by car from Cam Ranh Airport (CXR). Domestic flights connect to Cam Ranh Airport from Ho Chi Minh City (65 minutes) or Hanoi (105 minutes).

Vinh Hy Bay

Stunning bay location with direct access to pristine beaches and crystal-clear waters, surrounded by the natural beauty of Nui Chua National Park.

National Park

Embraced by Nui Chua National Park, offering unique opportunities for nature exploration, hiking, and wildlife observation.

Khanh Hoa Province

Located in the beautiful Khanh Hoa Province, 75 minutes from Cam Ranh Airport, offering easy access while maintaining complete seclusion.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Are there activities for children at the resort?

Yes, we have a dedicated kids' club with fun and educational activities for children of all ages, as well as family-friendly amenities like child care services, family pools, and guided nature tours designed for young guests.

What dining options are available at the resort?

Our resort features multiple dining options, including a fine dining restaurant, casual poolside lounges, and in-room dining. We offer a variety of international cuisines and locally inspired dishes, all curated by our team of renowned chefs.

Can I host a wedding or event at the resort?

Absolutely! We specialize in hosting luxurious weddings and private events. Our experienced events team will assist with everything from planning the decor and menu to coordinating with local vendors and ensuring every detail is perfect for your special day.

Are there activities and excursions available at the resort?

Yes, we offer a wide range of activities and excursions, from water sports like snorkeling, scuba diving, and kayaking to land-based activities such as guided hikes, golf, and cultural tours. We also provide private experiences, including yacht charters and sunset cruises.

Can I request early check-in or late check-out?

We offer early check-in and late check-out options based on availability. Please contact our front desk or concierge team in advance to arrange these services, or request during your booking for the best chance of availability.
